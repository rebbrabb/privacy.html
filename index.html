Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Neon Bonus Slot (“the App”). The App is a free-to-play entertainment game intended for users 18+ and does not involve real-money gambling.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process personal information such as your name, address, phone number, payment details, or precise location.

2. Local Device Data

The App may save game settings or progress locally on your device. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services that collect personal data.

4. In-App Purchases

The App contains no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no paid content.

5. Real-Money Gambling

The App does not offer real-money gambling. Any virtual currency or points in the App have no real-world value and cannot be redeemed for money or items of value.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
